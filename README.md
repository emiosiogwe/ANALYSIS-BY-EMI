# 📊 First Bank Loan Analysis

**Storytelling with Data**

A comprehensive SQL-based analysis of loan applications to assess lending risk and approval patterns.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

This analysis examines First Bank's loan application data to:
- Identify key factors influencing loan approval decisions
- Assess credit risk across applicant demographics
- Provide actionable recommendations for lending strategy

- **SALIU TERESSA FIRST BANK RECOMMENDATIONS.pdf** — Executive summary & recommendations

---

## 🔍 Analysis Highlights

### Key Queries Included:

1. **Loan Approval Rates** — Overall approval vs. rejection percentages
2. **Credit History Impact** — How credit scores influence approval decisions
3. **Demographic Analysis** — Approval patterns by gender, employment status, and property area
4. **Financial Metrics** — Average income, loan amounts, and terms by approval status
5. **Risk Scoring Model** — Categorizes applicants as HIGH RISK or NORMAL based on:
   - Credit history (0 = bad credit)
   - Loan amount (above average)
   - Employment status (self-employed)
   - Co-applicant income (presence/absence)

6. **Risk Distribution** — Cross-tabulation of risk categories vs. approval outcomes

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

- **Database**: MySQL
- **Language**: SQL

## 💡 Key Insights

- Credit history is a primary determinant of loan approval
- Self-employed applicants show different approval patterns
- Property area and applicant income correlate with loan decisions
- Risk-flagged applicants have distinct approval outcomes
